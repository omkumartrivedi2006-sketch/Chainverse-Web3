How this works

The miner computes SHA-256(prevHash + data + nonce) over and over, increasing the nonce each time, until the resulting hash starts with "00" — a tiny proof-of-work. Edit Block #1's data after mining and Block #2 immediately turns red: because its previous-hash no longer matches, the chain is broken.
